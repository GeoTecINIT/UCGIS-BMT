Frequently Asked Questions


What can be done with this tool?

The BMT allows to compare two BoK-annotated resources.


What is a BoK-annotated resource?

All resources created with EO4GEO tools (i.e., occupational profiles, job offers, educational offers) are automatically annotated when created.
Any other file following the Annotation Guidelines (v1.2) can also be understood by the BMT and can be used for comparison.


What examples of a comparison can be done?

For example, a university could compare an academic study program with an occupational profile, to discover how much of the knowledge required for the occupational profile is covered by the study program - and possibly adjust the study program to remedy missing knowledge. Or, a graduate could compare a job offer with his graduate school program, to discover which knowledge heâ€™s missing, and determine a VET course to complement this knowledge. Or, one could annotate a pdf file according to the guidelines (for example, a scientific article) and compare it with another resource (for example, a course described in the CDT tool), to determine suitability of the article to serve as reading assignment.
